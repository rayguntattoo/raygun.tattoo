owner

Orange County native specializing in freelance art design since his first job designing skateboards and shirts for Alva in the 80's. Along with his identical twin brother Ryan the two have won countless awards for their involvement in art competitions and business designs and undertook what would be there lifes work in 1989 when they began tattooing. Leading to the oppurtunity of owning and operating their own tattoo business "Raygun Tattoo" in Dana Point California in 1993. Currently operating under Raygun Productions, Craig has been doing a variety of designs in various mediums not limited to: custom tattooing, graphic design for clothing companies, stage set design, mask making, sculpture, and all types of fine art for such companies as:
